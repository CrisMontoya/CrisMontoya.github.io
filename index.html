                                                                        Cristhian Montoya

Welcome on my web page ! I am Professor at Departamento de Ciencias Matemáticas, EAFIT.

Formerly, I used to be a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Dubrovnik, Croatia, under the supervision of Martin Lazar. Previously, I got a Postdoctoral Researcher of Applied Mathematics, at Institute of Mathematical and Computational Engineering at Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile (PUC), under the supervision of Eduardo Cerpa . In addition, my first postdoctoral position (2016-2017) was done at Institute of Mathematics at Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México with Luz de Teresa and I got my Ph.D in 2016 in Engineering Sciences Mathematical Modelling at Universidad de Chile with Axel Osses like my advisor. During my doctoral studies, I was at Laboratoiré Jacques-Louis Lions (Paris, France (2015-2016)) with Sergio Guerrero like my mentor. I completed my master studies at Universidad de Santiago de Chile in 2012, and my undergraduate studies in mathematics at Universidad de Cauca, Colombia.
