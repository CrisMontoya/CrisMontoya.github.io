Welcome on my web page ! Since 2015, I am assistant professor at Laboratoire Jean Kuntzmann.

Formerly, I used to be a postdoctoral researcher at the Mathematics Department, at Rutgers, New Brunswick, under the supervision of Michael Vogelius.

Even before, I used to be a Ph.D. student at Laboratoire Jacques-Louis Lions under the joint supervision of Grégoire Allaire and Pascal Frey, and with Marc Albertelli, from Renault.
